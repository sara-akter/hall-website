Shahid Janani Jahanara Imam Hall is one of the ladies hostel of Mawlana Bhashani Science and Technology University(MBSTU). It is located in Santosh area of Tangail district.It was established in 2011 according to the name of Jahanara Imam.This hall is opened with a total number of 250 students on June 7, 2012.

There are total 74 rooms and 296 seats in this hall.Total number of students is now 350.
